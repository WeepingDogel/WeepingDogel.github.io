AM I WRONG?
A sad experience in childhood...
2021-03-15 00:24 folder Other   label 回忆  

When opened the vscode and sit in front of my computer, trying to write and post an article on my blog, I suddenly forgot what to write. There were only one sentence running in my mind, from left to right. It echoes everyday, every moment..

“AM I WRONG?”

In regard to the answer…

I don’t know… Just looking back on the past… And thinking whether it had led to by my fault..

I don’t know whether it was my fault that I was born with illness and low body protection in the biggest developing country. What I still remember today is that the illness was always my friend in childhood, exactly, before 10 years old.

I could only live and sleep in the hospital in most time.

And the few time of primary school was also terrible…

Especially, I will never forget my first day to go to primary school. I had the fever and the teacher was talking something I couldn’t understand.

(Something about the future, lifelong, universities, etc.)

While she was talking, I was so tired that I couldn’t concentrate on listening to her. Then she thought that I didn’t respect her. Slowly, she walked to me.

Suddenly she took up my sharpener and threw down! I was shocked by that. But for a while, I tried to pick up the broken sharpener. Suddenly, again, the teacher shouted at me, “It’s broken! Stupid kid!”. As the sound came out, all of the students in the classroom stared at me with an insulting eyesight. I wanted to cry but I have no force to do because of struggling the pain from the fever…

So, AM I WRONG? Is my fault? About all the things?
