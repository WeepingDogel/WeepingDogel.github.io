Guessing the selections on the paper, there was only one idea that I wanted to destroy the fucking campus.

With the time going, it was only anxious spirits that ran around my mind.

Sleepy and a little mad, I fell asleep while guessing and writing the shit “A B C D”, and suddenly woke up again and again.

Surely, I admit that I got trapped in the absent-minded stats, which made me buried in the temporary dreams. And I knew I failed the shit math exam again.

However, I don’t accept that everything is my fault. Even if I have no intention to debate the fucking topic about that, I still want to say: I have no choice because of the lack of the knowledge. Even I haven’t hated math in the pretty past years.

Even I I know that I am potential now, and even it is my laziness that prevents me now! I still want to push forward a statement to the public, which is the best expression to shout that I am eager to get encouragement, real encouragement, not flattering.

Additionally, the determination of taking control of English keeps me moving forward. It is English that makes me have power over seizing the scores back. Abundant confidence based on English abilities powerfully makes me be keen on showing my middle finger to the schooling.

And finally, the result is truly like a shit: I just got 8 points at math subject…

But I can’t be sad and prevented by the shit examination…. That’s because I still have a long way to go.
